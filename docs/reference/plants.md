# PvZ1 Plant Catalogue

Comprehensive reference of all plants in Plants vs. Zombies (original/GOTY edition). Stats reflect the original PC/console release.

## Legend

- **Cost** — Sun cost to plant
- **Recharge** — Time before the plant can be placed again: Fast (~7.5s), Slow (~30s), Very Slow (~50s)
- **Damage** — Damage per hit or effect type; Normal = 20 damage per pea equivalent
- **Range** — Effective range of the plant's attack or effect
- **Unlock** — Level at which the plant becomes available (Adventure mode)
